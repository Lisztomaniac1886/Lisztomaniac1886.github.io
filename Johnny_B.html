Johnny B
Portrayed by: Chad Rook
Appears in: Shadow of a Doubt
Died: 2014
Status: Deceased
Home: White Pine Bay, Oregon
Occupation: Drug dealer
Others: Nick Ford (boss)†

Johnny B was a young man who worked for Nick Ford's drug empire. Believing the latter had something to do with Gil Turner's death, Zane killed Johnny in retaliation.
