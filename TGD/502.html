Piece of Cake

"Piece of Cake" is the second episode of Season 5 of The Good Doctor. It aired on October 4, 2021. Read more about this episode on The Good Doctor Wiki (click here).

Shaun and the team race to save a pregnant woman’s baby and find her current situation as a convicted felon complicates it even more than they realize. Meanwhile the hospital take on some unexpected changes that the staff are not prepared for.
