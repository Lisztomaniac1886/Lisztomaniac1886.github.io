The Family

"The Family" is the eleventh episode of Season 5 of The Good Doctor. It will air on March 21, 2022. Read more about this episode on The Good Doctor Wiki (click here).



Dr. Shaun Murphy makes a personal connection with a precocious young patient which threatens his objectivity. Meanwhile, while volunteering at a long-term care facility, Dr. Morgan Reznick realizes she could help Dr. Aaron Glassman’s clinic by offering telemedicine services for nursing homes and care facilities.
